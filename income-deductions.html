2. Income & Deductions

This page focuses on the deductions subtracted from your gross pay to calculate your net pay. Deductions include federal and state taxes, FICA taxes, Medicare, health insurance, life insurance, and retirement contributions.

The deductions are based on your gross pay of $4,634.25. Benefits such as health insurance and life insurance are included in the deductions. Additionally, a 5% retirement contribution is calculated to ensure savings for the future.

Always consider purchasing personal life insurance outside of your job to maintain coverage even when changing employers.
