8. Entertainment & Hobbies

Entertainment & Hobbies

Entertainment and hobbies are important to maintain balance and enjoy life. Here's my plan for managing these activities within my budget:

  • Hulu subscription: $5.99/month
  • Netflix subscription: $7.99/month
  • PlayStation Plus subscription: $9.99/month
  • Gym membership: $15/month
  • Hair and nails (monthly budget): $30/month

Strategies: I am looking for cost-effective options, such as discounted gym memberships and streaming services, while maintaining my entertainment. I prioritize saving for big events, like concerts, by setting aside money monthly.

Explanation & Savings

I chose these activities and hobbies because they bring balance to my life, provide entertainment, and support my personal growth. I’ve budgeted carefully to ensure I’m enjoying my free time without overspending. Additionally, I’m saving for larger events like concerts and sports tickets, which allows me to avoid unexpected costs later on.
