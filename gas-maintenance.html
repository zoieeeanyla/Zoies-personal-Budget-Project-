Monthly Driving Calculation

Estimated your monthly driving costs:

  • Round Trip from Home to Work: 7.5 miles one way, 15 miles per day, 5 days/week, 4 weeks = 300 miles/month.
  • Additional Monthly Driving (groceries, entertainment, etc.): 100 miles/month.
  • Total Monthly Miles: 400 miles/month.

Gasoline Budget

With my 2015 Nissan Sentra SV, which averages 30 MPG:

  • Gallons Needed: 400 miles / 30 MPG = 13.33 gallons/month.
  • Average Gas Price: $3.20 per gallon.
  • Monthly Gas Budget: 13.33 gallons x $3.20 x2 = $84.34.

Maintenance Costs

The estimated costs for regular maintenance based on my vehicle model:

  • Tires Replacement (every 2 years): $300
  • Brakes Replacement (every 2 years): $200
  • Total Maintenance Costs (over 2 years): $500
  • Monthly Maintenance Savings: $500 / 24 months = $20.83/month.

Gas and Maintenance Budget

Total monthly gas and maintenance cost:

  • Total Monthly Cost: $84.34 (gas) + $20.83 (maintenance) = $106.17
