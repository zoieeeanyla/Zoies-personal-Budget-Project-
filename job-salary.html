1. Job & Salary

\

Job Title: Health Education Specialist

\

Company: Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC)

\

Annual Salary: $55,611

\

Monthly Salary: $4,634.25

\

\ Health Education Specialists design and implement strategies to improve the health and well-being of individuals and communities. They educate people on health topics such as nutrition, fitness, disease prevention, and wellness.\

\

Education Requirements: A bachelor\'92s degree in health education, public health, or a related field. Certification such as the CHES (Certified Health Education Specialist) is often required.
